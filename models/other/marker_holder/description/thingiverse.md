A simple, lightweight wall-mounted holder for markers and pens. Tapered open-bottom design grips the marker by its sides. Keeps your wall or whiteboard clean and uncluttered.

**Key Features**
- Wider opening at the top for easy insertion.
- Open bottom — the marker is held by the tapered walls, not a shelf. Uses less filament and prints faster.
- Narrow side with 13 mm inner diameter keeps markers snugly in place. Scale in the slicer to fit different sizes.
- Flat back for flush wall mounting with double-sided tape.
- Smooth filleted edges for a clean, polished look.
- Easy to print vertically with no supports needed.

A convenient storage box for a complete set of 12 international travel power adapters. Designed specifically for Ceptics individual plug adapters ([Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B01DJEBLQU)), but might work with similar adapter sets. Each adapter sits in its own recess with the socket type (A through N) engraved on the bottom. Makes a great gift for frequent travelers.

**Key Features**
- 12 recesses arranged in a 6x2 grid — one for each international socket type (A, B, C, D, E/F, G, H, I, J, L, M, N).
- Engraved socket type labels on the floor of each recess for quick sorting, while labels on the adapters help pick the right one for your destination.
- Snap-lock cantilever lid that clicks securely shut and opens without tools.
- "Travel Adapter Set" text engraved on the lid top.
- Tapered box walls for a clean look and easier lid alignment.
- Prints as two parts (box and lid) with minimal supports needed.

**Dimensions**
- Box: ~250 x 100 x 12 mm (base tray)
- Lid: ~250 x 100 x 76 mm

**Printing**
- Lid: PETG recommended — the snap-lock cantilevers need some flexibility to avoid breaking.
- Box: ABS works perfectly, but other materials should be fine too.

**Compatibility**
Designed for Ceptics 12-piece individual adapter set. Individual plug adapters are more reliable than universal all-in-one adapters, and this box keeps them organized and ready for any trip.
